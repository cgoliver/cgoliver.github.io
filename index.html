Carlos Oliver Homepage

Assistant Professor in the Department of Molecular Physiology and Biophysics and core, and Department of Computer Science, core member of the Center for Applied AI in Protein Dynamics at Vanderbilt University.

Previously, I led the Molecular Pattern Mining Project Group at the Department of Machine Learning and Systems Biology at the Max Planck Institute of Biochemistry, postdoc at ETH Zurich and a member of the labs of Jérôme Waldispühl and William L. Hamilton during my PhD studies in Computer Science.

I build algorithms that extract knowledge from complex biological systems. More on my research here.
