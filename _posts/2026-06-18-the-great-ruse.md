Do not waste <br>
a single moment <br>
shouting into the dark.<br>

For one day <br>
the angels behind you<br>
will stop singing.<br>

And you will turn <br>
and find nothing <br>
worth defending. <br>

Know that the devil's game <br>
is undoing. <br>

And all it takes for him <br>
is to hold your <br>
gaze.

Your game <br>
is to prune <br>
the roses. <br>

Cultivate so much <br>
beauty<br>
and stillness <br>
in your life<br>
that when the barbarians<br>
break through<br>
and burn it all,<br>
God will shed a tear<br>
and the demons will shudder<br>
at the light<br>
of your glorious flame.

For one fleeting instant,<br>
the devil rejoiced<br>
at his triumph<br>
over the spotless Man <br>
on the Cross, <br>
only to release a mortal cry<br>
when he looked up <br>
and saw <br>
that he delivered<br>
the perfect offering<br>
to God.
