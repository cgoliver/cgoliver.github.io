Do not spend<br>
too much time<br>
on the demons.<br>

I know water <br>
is rising.

I know the guard<br>
is sleeping.

But know that <br>
the devil's game <br>
is in undoing. <br>

And all it takes <br>
is to hold your gaze <br>
just long enough <br>
for the angels  <br>
behind you<br>
to quit singing.

Your game <br>
is to prune <br>
the roses. 

Cultivate so much <br>
beauty<br>
and stillness <br>
in your life<br>
that when the barbarians<br>
break through<br>
and burn it all,<br>
God will shed a tear<br>
and the demons will shudder<br>
at the light.

This is the great secret.

The devil rejoiced<br>
at the spotless Man <br>
on the Cross. <br>

Until a mortal cry <br>
overcame him <br>
when he saw <br>
that by his hand <br>
was delivered <br>
a perfect offering <br>
to God. <br>
