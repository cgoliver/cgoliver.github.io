Do not waste <br>
a single moment <br>
on the demons.<br>

For the angels<br>
behind you will<br>
grow silent.<br>

Know that <br>
the devil's game <br>
is in undoing. <br>

And all it takes for him<br>
is to hold your <br>
gaze.

Your game <br>
is to prune <br>
the roses. <br>

Cultivate so much <br>
beauty<br>
and stillness <br>
in your life<br>
that when the barbarians<br>
break through<br>
and burn it all,<br>
God will shed a tear<br>
and the demons will shudder<br>
at the light<br>
of your glorious flame.

This is the great secret.

The devil rejoiced<br>
at the spotless Man <br>
on the Cross. <br>

Until a mortal cry <br>
overcame him <br>
and he saw <br>
that by his hand <br>
was delivered <br>
a perfect offering to God. <br>
